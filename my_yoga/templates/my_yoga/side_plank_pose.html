Vasisthasana (Side Plank Pose)

Vasisthasana, or Side Plank Pose, is an intermediate to advanced yoga pose that strengthens the arms, wrists, and core muscles. It also improves balance and concentration.

Steps:

  1. 1. Start in a plank position with your wrists directly under your shoulders and your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  2. 2. Shift your weight onto your right hand and the outer edge of your right foot. Stack your left foot on top of your right foot.
  3. 3. Engage your core and lift your left arm up towards the ceiling, creating a straight line from your head to your heels.
  4. 4. Hold the pose for several breaths, then return to the plank position and switch sides.
