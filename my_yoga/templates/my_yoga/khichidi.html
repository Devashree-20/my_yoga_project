Khichidi Recipe

Khichidi is a nutritious and comforting dish that's easy to prepare. Here's a simple recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Lentils (such as yellow mung dal)
  • Vegetables (such as carrots, peas, and potatoes)
  • Spices (such as turmeric, cumin, and mustard seeds)
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash rice and lentils thoroughly.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker.
  3. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  5. Add washed rice and lentils, along with turmeric and salt. Stir well.
  6. Add water (ratio of rice:lentils:water is typically 1:1:3) and pressure cook until done.
  7. Serve hot with a dollop of ghee or yogurt.
