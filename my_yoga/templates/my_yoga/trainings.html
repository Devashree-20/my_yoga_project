Yoga Therapy

Do you suffer from any of the below ailments?

Don’t Worry. It can be cured by correct & continuous yoga therapy. Our senior yoga therapist will get you out of this quickly!

Back-Pain

These days, Back pain is a very common problem among working professionals and elders. People who sit & work all day long without breaks, who travel frequently, who follows improper ergonomics suffers from back pain.Our trainers can help you to relieve pain completely &with very simple yet effective yoga poses and other practices.

Neck-Pain

Neck pain has become a prevalent, primarily due to inactive work behaviors such as prolonged use of computers and inappropriate work postures.With a specialized approach, our trainers conducts an in-depth analysis to determine the root cause, the individual’s capabilities, and the severity of the problem, enabling him to tailor yoga practices to address your neck pain concerns.

Sleeping Disorder

Sleep apnea is an extremely dangerous sleep disorder. It stops and starts breathing over and over again while sleeping.Yoga therapy for sleep apnea improves the diaphragmatic breathing, toning the required muscles, weight reduction, improves quality of sleep.We offer sleep apnea treatment with our customized theraupatic yoga practice for individuals in order to help them to get better sleep and alleviate sleep disorders.
