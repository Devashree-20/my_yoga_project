Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Vrikshasana, or Tree Pose, is a standing yoga pose that improves balance, concentration, and strengthens the legs, ankles, and core muscles.

Steps:

  1. 1. Stand tall with your feet hip-width apart and arms by your sides.
  2. 2. Shift your weight onto your left foot and lift your right foot off the ground.
  3. 3. Place the sole of your right foot against the inner left thigh or calf, avoiding the knee.
  4. 4. Bring your hands together in front of your chest in a prayer position or extend them overhead.
  5. 5. Gaze softly at a fixed point to help maintain balance.
  6. 6. Hold the pose for several breaths, then repeat on the other side.
