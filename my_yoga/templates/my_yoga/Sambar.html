Sambar Recipe

Sambar is a popular South Indian dish, typically served with rice, idli, dosa, or vada. It's made with toor dal (pigeon pea), vegetables, tamarind, and a blend of spices. Here's a simple recipe to make delicious sambar:

Ingredients:

  • Toor dal (pigeon pea)
  • Vegetables (such as drumsticks, carrots, potatoes, and beans)
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Sambar powder
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Asafoetida (hing)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook toor dal until soft. Mash it and keep aside.
  2. Boil vegetables with turmeric powder until tender.
  3. Soak tamarind in water and extract pulp.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add boiled vegetables, tamarind pulp, sambar powder, salt, and mashed dal. Cook until flavors blend.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with rice or idli.
