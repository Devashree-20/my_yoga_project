Bhramari (Bee Breath)

Bhramari, or Bee Breath, is a calming pranayama (breathing exercise) that helps reduce stress and anxiety. It also improves concentration and soothes the nervous system.

Steps:

  1. 1. Sit comfortably in a quiet place.
  2. 2. Close your eyes and take a few deep breaths to relax.
  3. 3. Place your index fingers on your ears, thumbs on your temples, and remaining fingers lightly over your closed eyes.
  4. 4. Inhale deeply through your nose.
  5. 5. Exhale slowly while making a humming sound like a bee.
  6. 6. Repeat for several rounds, focusing on the calming vibration in your head and throat.
