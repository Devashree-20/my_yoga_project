VEGETABLE SOUP RECIPE

Vegetable soup is a nutritious and delicious dish that is easy to prepare. Here's a simple recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, celery, onions, potatoes, etc.)
  • Vegetable broth or water
  • Herbs and spices (salt, pepper, thyme, bay leaf, etc.)
  • Optional: diced tomatoes, beans, pasta, or rice for added flavor and texture

Instructions:

  1. Chop the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and sauté until they begin to soften.
  4. Pour in enough vegetable broth or water to cover the vegetables.
  5. Season with herbs and spices according to your taste.
  6. Simmer the soup for about 20-30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Optional: Add any additional ingredients like diced tomatoes, beans, pasta, or rice.
  8. Adjust seasoning as needed and serve hot.

Enjoy your nutritious and comforting vegetable soup!
