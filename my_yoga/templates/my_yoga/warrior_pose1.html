Virabhadrasana I (Warrior Pose I)

Virabhadrasana I, or Warrior Pose I, is a foundational yoga pose that strengthens the legs, opens the hips, and improves balance and focus.

Steps:

  1. 1. Begin in a standing position at the front of your mat with your feet hip-width apart.
  2. 2. Step your left foot back about 3-4 feet, keeping your feet parallel to each other.
  3. 3. Bend your right knee, aligning it directly over your right ankle, and lower your hips towards the floor.
  4. 4. Extend your arms overhead, reaching up towards the sky with your fingertips.
  5. 5. Hold the pose for several breaths, then switch sides and repeat with your left leg forward.
