Golden Milk Recipe

Golden Milk, also known as Turmeric Milk, is a traditional Ayurvedic drink with various health benefits. Here's how to make it:

  1. Heat 1 cup of milk (dairy or plant-based) in a small saucepan.
  2. Add 1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric, a pinch of black pepper, and a pinch of cinnamon.
  3. Optional: Add a small piece of fresh ginger for extra flavor.
  4. Stir well and bring to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Remove from heat and let cool slightly before serving.
  7. Sweeten with honey or maple syrup to taste, if desired.

Enjoy your warm and soothing Golden Milk!
